3. LGPL §6 — written offer (for anyone who distributes a built SpiceMac.app)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

The LGPL-2.1+ libraries above are used as dynamically-linked frameworks, and the
GStreamer plugins are statically linked into the SpiceMac executable. To satisfy
LGPL-2.1 §6 when distributing a built .app you must make the corresponding source
available and allow relinking:

  * Source for each LGPL library: the exact upstream versions in the fetched
    sysroot (see scripts/fetch-sysroot.sh and the pinned versions).
  * Relinking: SpiceMac is open source — anyone can rebuild it against modified
    LGPL libraries (replace the frameworks under Contents/Frameworks/ and re-run
    scripts/build-app.sh, or rebuild from the app object files). This satisfies
    §6(a)/§6(b).

For a copy of the corresponding source or with any licensing question, open an
issue at https://github.com/Ching367436/spice-mac.
